How we work

We do short-stay turnovers across the Mornington Peninsula. The standard is simple: guest-ready, consistent, and on-time.

Checklists Photos on completion Issue reporting Reliable timing

Requirements

  • ABN (preferred) and ability to invoice
  • Public liability insurance (recommended)
  • Own transport (most jobs are not walkable)
  • Smartphone for photos + messaging
  • Police check (or willing to obtain)

Turnover standard (non-negotiables)

  • Arrive on time. Message immediately if delayed.
  • Bathrooms + kitchen reset to “hotel-ready” standard.
  • No hair/dust in corners; mirrors and taps polished.
  • Bins emptied, liners replaced (if provided), smells neutral.
  • Floors vacuumed/mopped appropriately.
  • Quick staging: cushions straight, surfaces tidy, lights as instructed.
  • Completion photos: key areas + any issue close-ups.

Linen & restock

Linen/restock is job-specific. If included, you’ll be told in advance and provided clear instructions (where linen is stored, what gets replaced, and quantities).
